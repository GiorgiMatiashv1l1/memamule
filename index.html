FaQs

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Chat with our support team or email us at memamule.info@gmail.com

  • How do I input my soil data?

    → You can manually enter soil values like pH, nitrogen, phosphorus, etc., or upload a test report.

  • What do the analysis results mean?

    → Our AI breaks down your soil health and tells you what nutrients are missing and how to fix it.

  • Do I need a lab test first?

    → Not always. If you know your soil values, you can use them directly. For best results, we recommend a basic lab test.

  • Is this app suitable for all crops?

    → Yes! Our recommendations are tailored to the crops you select during setup.

  • How often should I analyze my soil?

    → Ideally once per growing season, or whenever you suspect poor plant performance.

  • Is this service free to use?

    → We offer both free and premium features. Basic soil insights are always free.
